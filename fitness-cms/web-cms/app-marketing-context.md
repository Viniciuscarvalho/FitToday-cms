# FitToday Marketing Context

## Product Description
FitToday is a comprehensive CMS and management platform designed specifically for Personal Trainers to professionalize their business, scale their workout delivery, and manage student relationships and payments.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Independent Personal Trainers looking to scale their online or hybrid coaching.
- **Secondary:** Small fitness studios or trainer teams.
- **Persona:** Professional, tech-savvy (or tech-seeking), growth-oriented, values time efficiency and professional image.

## Core Value Propositions
1. **Professionalism:** Offer a dedicated app for students (white-label feel).
2. **Scalability:** Build and manage hundreds of workout programs effortlessly.
3. **Financial Control:** Transparent subscription models for trainers with flexible payment flows for students.
4. **All-in-One:** CRM + Program Builder + Finance in one place.

## Key Features
- **Dynamic Program Builder:** Create workouts with series, reps, and media.
- **Student App:** Dedicated mobile experience for students to track progress.
- **Flexible Payment Flows:**
    - **Trainer -> FitToday:** Simple monthly subscription with tiered commissions.
    - **Student -> Trainer:** Direct payment (WhatsApp/Pix) or platform-facilitated (future). Currently emphasizes direct relationship.
- **Analytics:** Basic to advanced tracking of trainer revenue and student engagement.

## Competitors
- MFIT Personal
- Treino de Mestre
- Nexur
- Trainerize (international)

## Positioning
The most professional and modern platform for Brazilian Personal Trainers who want a "SaaS-like" experience for their own fitness business.

## Tone of Voice
- Professional but energetic.
- Empowering and supportive.
- Clean, modern, and high-tech (Webflow-inspired).
- Clear and transparent about costs.
